About Me

Background

I have been intrigued by technology from an early age. My parents ran a business that sold hardware and software solutions so as a family we stayed pretty current with emerging technology trends. I can still remember playing Zork and Space Invaders on some of those older machines.

In college I pursued business and attained an MBA with a Information Systems concentration. Since graduating nearly 5 years ago I have worked full time as a software developer. My experiences from my career as well as contributions to and observations of open source projects have helped me to advance my education. I am continually learning about best practices in software development and I endeavor to integrate them into my daily work.

Experience

More than any of the other projects in my portfolio I am most proud of my involvement with Wichita's local development community devICT . As an organizer I help to connect, empower, and educate a great group of developers and aspiring developers. My motivations in this regard are not completely altruistic, however, as I frequently learn from this talented group.

Focus

I am comfortable with all layers of the web stack but the work I prefer is on the back end and in operations. I am very interested in developing truly RESTful APIs, Service Oriented Architecture, and Test Driven Development. I love automation, virtualization, and efficient development workflows that empower (rather than hinder) developers. I grew up on PHP but I also enjoy Python, Ruby, and Node.js. I have plans to expand my skillset to include more systems languages like Go and functional languages like Haskell.

View my portfolio or my resume to read more about my experiences. I also blog about some of the things I have learned or found useful.
