TimeIPS

php python javascript bash c++ doctrine silex angular less jenkins tdd rest

From October 2010 to February 2015 the majority of my development focus has been with my employer TimeIPS. We made a time and attendance system which is used by thousands of companies around the world.

TimeIPS is used to

  • Track employee hours worked from a variety of hardware and software interfaces
  • Record benefit usages and accruals
  • Monitor employee adherence to schedules
  • Ensure employee coverage for particular tasks or locations
  • Calculate FLSA overtime
  • Record piecework pay such as tips and bonuses
  • Serve company documents to employees and managers
  • Calculate automatic holiday rules
  • And many other things

At TimeIPS I lead a team of 4 in the development of our products. Some of my major accomplishments in this role include:

  • Introducing Test Driven Development with PHPUnit
  • Implementing Continuous Integration with Jenkins-CI
  • Starting code reviews via Phabricator
  • Leading the development of a REST API
  • Working to update legacy code to use the Doctrine 2 ORM
  • Implementing a Zero Defect policy
  • Developing a system for managing our farm of QEMU virtual machines
  • Revamped our build process making heavy use of Composer, Gulp, and Bower
