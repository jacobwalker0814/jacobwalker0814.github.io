devICT

devICT is a local development community that covers all aspects of development. I first joined the group in December of 2012 and have made it a priority to be actively involved. I help to organize events, find and engage new members, and give presentations.

The group's home on the web is devict.org . The site was previously hosted on Heroku but has since been moved to a VPS. The application runs on Rails. I have made several contributions to the site such as setting up infrastructure, the user management system, and the IRC users integration.
